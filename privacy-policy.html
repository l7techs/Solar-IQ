Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the SolarIQ mobile application ("SolarIQ", "the app", "we") collects, uses and protects information when you use it. SolarIQ is published by Level7 Tech and is a third-party client for Voltronic / AXPERT KING II hybrid solar inverters. SolarIQ is not affiliated with Voltronic Power or Shinemonitor.

1. Data we collect

SolarIQ collects only the minimum data it needs to function:

  • Shinemonitor username and password — stored encrypted in your private Supabase account so the app can authenticate against the Shinemonitor API on your behalf.
  • Inverter telemetry — PV power, load power, battery state-of-charge, battery power, grid power, daily energy, device fault codes.
  • Home location (optional) — latitude and longitude you pick on the map, used only for local solar irradiance calculations.
  • Push notification token — Firebase Cloud Messaging (Android) or Apple Push Notification service (iOS) token, used to deliver alerts.
  • App telemetry — app version, platform (iOS/Android), and preference settings.

2. How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to run the features of the app: displaying live system status, rendering charts, computing cost and ROI analytics, and sending push notifications you opted in to. We do not sell, rent or share your data with advertisers. We do not run third-party advertising SDKs inside the app.

3. Where your data is stored

User accounts, systems and readings are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security — every account can only read rows that belong to it. Push tokens live only as long as your install is active. Locally, the app keeps a small SQLite cache of recent readings for offline charts; this cache is wiped on sign-out or account deletion.

4. Third-party services

  • Shinemonitor cloud — required to talk to the inverter.
  • Supabase — authentication, database, edge functions.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification service — delivery of push notifications.

These providers process your data only to deliver the corresponding feature. Their own privacy policies apply to data they handle on our behalf.

5. Your rights and choices

You can review all the data tied to your account from the Settings screen inside the app. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is irreversible and cascades to every row we hold for you — systems, readings, push tokens and preferences.

6. Security

All network traffic is encrypted with TLS. Credentials are never logged. The app does not transmit your data to any destination outside the services listed in section 4.

7. Children

SolarIQ is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be announced inside the app.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email level7tech@gmail.com.
