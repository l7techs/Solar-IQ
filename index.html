How it works

Three steps. Ten minutes. Done.

FAQ

Common questions

Which inverters are supported?

Voltronic / AXPERT KING II hybrid inverters that are registered on the Shinemonitor cloud. Single and dual-inverter setups are both supported.

Do I need to install anything on the inverter?

No. If your inverter is already online in the Shinemonitor mobile/web app, Solar-IQ will work immediately with the same credentials.

Is my Shinemonitor password safe?

Yes. It's stored encrypted in your private Supabase account, protected by row-level security, and used only to authenticate the read-only polling calls.

Does the app work offline?

Live readings need an internet connection. Historical charts are cached locally so you can scroll them offline.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Delete Account. You'll be asked to type the word DELETE to confirm. All account data is permanently removed.

Is it really free?

Yes. No ads, no trackers, no subscription. Built by L7 Techs for the community.
